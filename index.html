You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

To win the game you must match your total score to the random number.
If your total score exceeds the random number, then you lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

The value of each crystal will change with every new game.
